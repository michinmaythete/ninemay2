About Pune

Pune, also known as Poona, is a major city in Maharashtra. It is known for its historical significance, pleasant weather, educational institutions, and vibrant culture.

Famous Landmarks

  • Shaniwar Wada
  • Sinhagad Fort
  • Aga Khan Palace
  • Pataleshwar Cave Temple

Culture

Pune is a hub of classical music, theatre, literature, and festivals. Ganesh Utsav is celebrated with great enthusiasm across the city.

Educational Hub

Often called the "Oxford of the East", Pune is home to renowned institutions like Savitribai Phule Pune University, Fergusson College, and Symbiosis International University.
