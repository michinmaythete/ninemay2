Ancient History

India is home to one of the world's oldest civilizations, the Indus Valley Civilization, and has a deep-rooted history dating back thousands of years.

Culture & Traditions

India is known for its diverse cultures, languages, religions, and festivals like Diwali, Holi, Eid, and Christmas, which showcase unity in diversity.

Heritage Monuments

India is dotted with architectural marvels such as the Taj Mahal, Qutub Minar, and Konark Sun Temple that reflect its glorious past.

Freedom Struggle

The Indian freedom struggle led by Mahatma Gandhi, Bhagat Singh, Rani Lakshmibai and many others, ended colonial rule and brought independence in 1947.
